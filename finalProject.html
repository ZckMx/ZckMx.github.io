My program is a fun and short version of the popular game show, "Are You Smarter than a 5th Grader? The game is simple, you answer five questions that a fifth grader should be able to get correct, and if you get at least four out of the five correct, you win! To start the game, click the "START" button with the mouse, and then use the arrow keys on your keyboard to direct the bitmoji character through the bottom hemisphere of the square with the answer you would like to select. Goodluck!
